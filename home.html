If it’s good coffee you seek at UCSD, then Muir Woods is the answer. We carry fair trade coffee from a local roaster, organic loose leaf teas, and a selection of pastries from a local bakery. Best on campus! We serve our products with a smile and as much conversation as you'd like.

Muir Woods carries organic coffee from local vendors and a wide variety of loose leaf teas.  We also offer many alternatives in preparation like soy milk or almond milk on request. On-campus students fear not! We accept Triton Cash.
